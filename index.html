Why Are Glaciers Melting?

Glaciers are melting mostly as a result of human-induced global warming. Carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases increase global temperatures by trapping heat in the atmosphere. As a result, glaciers and ice sheets are losing mass rapidly, leading to rising sea levels and altered ecosystems.

What We Can Do

  • Ending our reliance on fossil fuels.
  • Promote energy efficiency in homes.
  • Advocate for stronger climate policies and regulations that limit greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Reach out to your local elected officials to find out if your city has a disaster response plan for right now.
  • Raise awareness and educate others on the significance of taking action to protect glaciers and prevent climate change.
